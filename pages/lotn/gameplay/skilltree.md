updated: July 19, 2026
---

Skills increase by performing related activities.

Examples include:
- Dealing damage with weapons
- Blocking attacks
- Wearing armor during combat
- Mining ores
- Cutting logs
- Fishing
- Crafting equipment

Skills begin at level **15** and can reach level **100**.

Use `/lotn skills` to view your skills and perk trees.
