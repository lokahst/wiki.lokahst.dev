---
title: Fast Travel
updated: July 19, 2026
---

Fast Travel allows players to save locations and return to them instantly.

Use `/lotn travel` to open the Fast Travel menu.

Players can save up to **3 spawnpoints**.

To save a spawnpoint:
1. Stand on a safe solid block.
2. Make sure there are two clear blocks above it.
3. Select an empty slot in the Fast Travel menu.

Fast Travel cannot be used while the player is in combat.

A destination cannot be used if it becomes unsafe or if its world is no longer available.
