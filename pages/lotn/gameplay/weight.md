---
title: Weight
updated: July 19, 2026
---

Weapons, armor, tools, food, and potions have weight.

Your current carried weight includes items in:
- Your inventory
- Armor slots

The default carrying capacity is **200 kg**.

## Weight Penalties
| Carried Weight | Effect |
|----------------|--------|
| ~91% capacity | Slower Stamina regeneration |
| Above capacity | Sprinting is disabled |
| Far above capacity | Slowness is applied |
