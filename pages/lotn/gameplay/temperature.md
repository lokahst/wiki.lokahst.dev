---
title: Temperature
updated: July 19, 2026
---

LOTN calculates the player's temperature based on the surrounding environment.

Temperature is affected by:
- Biome
- Month
- Time of day
- Weather
- Altitude
- Nearby heat sources
- Leather armor

Cold temperatures reduce Stamina and Magicka regeneration.

Warm temperatures improve regeneration.

## Heat Sources
Nearby heat sources include:
- Campfires
- Fire
- Lava
- Magma blocks
- Furnaces
- Torches
- Lanterns

Higher locations are colder, while deep underground areas receive a small temperature increase.
Leather armor slows down how quickly the player becomes cold.
