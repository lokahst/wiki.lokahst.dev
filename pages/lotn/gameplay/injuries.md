---
title: Injuries
updated: July 19, 2026
---

A broken leg may happen after taking enough fall damage.

While your leg is broken:
- Movement speed is reduced
- Sprinting is disabled
- The injury remains after death so you wont skip it

Broken legs can be healed using an plugin's health potion.

Endurance reduces the chance of receiving an injury.

Natural healing is disabled by default. If enabled, it takes 2 in-game days to heal.
