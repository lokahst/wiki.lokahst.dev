---
title: Friends
updated: July 19, 2026
---

You can find friends through **Player Search**, type online player's name and send them friend request, or in **Your stats**, click on nametag **Add friend (Chat)**.

You can disable/enable friend request in **Your Stats** GUI, as **Friend requests**, or see your friends in the same GUI
in **My Friends**.

To use friends system, you need permission `valentines.friends.use`. To add friends, you need permission `valentines.friends.add`.
