This system adds 17 in-plugin achievements for better gameplay. Achievements are automatically unlocked while player is interacting with plugin or with other players.

## Achievements
### First Kiss Received
- Receive your first kiss from your partner (other player)

### First Hug Received
- Receive your first hug from your partner (other player)

### First Like Received
- Receive your first hug from your partner (other player)

### First Kiss Given
- Kiss someone

### First Hug Given
- Hug someone

### First Like Given
- Send a like to someone

### Kissable
- Receive 10 kisses

### Huggable
- Receive 10 hugs

### Likeable
- Receive 10 likes

### Just Married
- Marry someone

### It's Complicated
- Divorce

### One Month Together
- Be married for 30 days

### One Year Anniversary
- Be married for 365 days

### Two Years Strong
- Be married for 2 years

### Mood Setter
- Set your mood for the first time

### New Bestie
- Find a friend

### 3-Month Bond
- Stay friends for atleast 3 months
